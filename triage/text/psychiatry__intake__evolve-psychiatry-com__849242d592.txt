                                                            FINANCIAL POLICY

Insurance Claims
As a courtesy, Evolve Psychiatry will submit claims to your health insurance provider for services rendered. However, patients are ultimately
responsible for all charges, including co-pays, deductibles, co-insurance amounts, and any services not covered by their insurance plan. Payment
of co-pays and known out-of-pocket expenses is required at the time of service. Regardless of insurance coverage, financial responsibility for the
full cost of treatment remains with the patient. While we make reasonable efforts to facilitate insurance reimbursement, we cannot guarantee
coverage or payment from your insurance company.

Patients are responsible for following up with their insurer regarding the status of any submitted claims. If a claim is denied or becomes the
patient’s financial responsibility, payment is due in full within 30 days of notification. All patients are required to complete our patient
registration form and provide a copy of a valid driver’s license and current insurance card prior to receiving services. Failure to provide accurate
and timely insurance information may result in denied claims and full financial liability for the services rendered. This includes claims denied due
to delayed or incorrect information resulting in “untimely filing.”

In cases where services are provided by a clinician operating under supervision, such as an associate-level therapist, nurse practitioner or
trainee, billing may be submitted under the supervising clinician’s name and credentials in accordance with applicable laws and payer policies.
This billing practice does not affect the scope or quality of care received.

By signing below, the patient authorizes Evolve Psychiatry to charge any outstanding balances, including missed appointment fees, to the
payment method on file in accordance with this Financial Policy. The patient acknowledges that individual transaction notices may not be
provided prior to each charge, but may request receipts or a full account summary through the patient portal at any time.


Referrals
It is the patient's responsibility to obtain and maintain all required referrals from their primary care physician or referring clinician, as mandated
by their insurance plan. Failure to secure a valid referral prior to receiving services may result in the patient being financially responsible for the
full cost of those services.
Evolve Psychiatry’s Patient Success Team may assist with obtaining referral documentation as a courtesy; however, this does not relieve the
patient of their obligation to ensure that valid referrals are in place. By choosing to receive services without a valid referral, the patient
acknowledges and accepts financial responsibility for all resulting charges.


Medicare
Evolve Psychiatry accepts Medicare Part B. Patients whose primary or sole coverage is Medicare are responsible for paying the applicable annual
deductible and the standard 20% co-insurance at the time of service.
If the patient has secondary insurance coverage, Evolve Psychiatry will submit claims to the secondary payor if complete and valid insurance
information is provided. Patients may be required to sign an Advance Beneficiary Notice (ABN) for services or procedures not covered by
Medicare. Patients retain the right to decline any such services and, if declined, will be asked to sign a waiver confirming their refusal at the time
of the appointment.


Self-Pay
A self-pay account applies to any patient who:

     ●​    Does not have active health insurance coverage;
     ●​    Holds insurance from a plan with which Evolve Psychiatry is not contracted;
     ●​    Fails to provide valid insurance documentation at the time of service.

Evolve Psychiatry does not accept attorney letters or agreements for contingency-based payment. It is the patient’s responsibility to confirm
whether Evolve Psychiatry participates in their specific insurance plan. In the absence of verifiable coverage, patients will be classified as
self-pay. Charges for self-pay services include, but are not limited to, time spent by the clinician on: clinical evaluation, records review, direct
patient encounters, prescription and lab orders, documentation, communication with third parties (e.g., schools, legal representatives, other
clinicians), and billing-related activities. A fee schedule is available upon request.


Non-Covered Services
Certain services provided by Evolve Psychiatry may not be covered or reimbursable by Medicare or other insurance carriers. This includes, but is
not limited to, services deemed “not medically necessary,” investigational, or excluded by the terms of the patient’s plan.

Patients are financially responsible for all non-covered services and must pay in full at the time of the visit. A list of commonly non-covered
services is available on the Evolve Psychiatry website and may be updated periodically. Patients are encouraged to verify coverage directly with
their insurance provider.


Credit Card Information
For your convenience, Evolve Psychiatry accepts major credit cards, bank-issued debit cards, and flexible spending (Flex Pay) cards. We do not
accept cash or personal checks.


Card-on-File Payment Policy
To streamline billing and reduce administrative costs, Evolve Psychiatry requires that each patient maintain an active debit or credit card on file
with the practice or with our designated payment processor. This card will be securely stored using industry-standard encryption and
tokenization protocols to protect your financial information.


Payment
Payment for all services is due at the time they are rendered unless prior arrangements have been made directly with the patient’s health
insurance carrier and confirmed by Evolve Psychiatry. Copayments, deductibles, and any known patient-responsible charges must be paid at the
time of service, as required by the patient’s insurance agreement.

If a patient is unable to pay the outstanding balance prior to their next scheduled appointment, Evolve Psychiatry reserves the right to
reschedule the appointment or refer the patient to another clinician.

The patient agrees to be financially responsible for all charges for services rendered that are not covered by their insurance plan. The patient
further authorizes Evolve Psychiatry, its affiliated entities, and/or designated payment processor to securely store payment card information and
to charge that card for any and all amounts owed, including but not limited to:

     ●​    Copayments;
     ●​    Coinsurance amounts after application of insurance benefits;
     ●​    Charges agreed to under a payment plan;
     ●​    Fees for missed appointments or late cancellations;
     ●​    Services or supplies not covered by insurance.

It is the patient’s responsibility to maintain accurate and current payment information to prevent billing issues or disruption in care.


Outstanding Balances / Collections
Prior to receiving additional services, any outstanding balance must be paid in full. Patients will either be notified of their outstanding balance at
the beginning of their next scheduled appointment or be charged automatically 30 days after the balance becomes due, whichever occurs first.
If an automatic charge fails or is not authorized, the patient may receive a monthly billing statement for the balance owed. Patients are expected
to pay their balance by the due date indicated on the statement to avoid additional interest, late fees, or disruption of care. Copies of all
transaction receipts will be maintained in the patient’s record and are accessible via the secure online patient portal.

Payment plans may be offered at the discretion of Evolve Psychiatry, depending on the patient’s financial circumstances and the nature of the
outstanding balance. Patients seeking a payment plan must contact the billing department in advance of the due date to discuss eligibility and
terms. All payment plan agreements must be documented in writing.

Balances that remain unpaid for more than 30 days may result in discharge from care, subject to the terms of Evolve Psychiatry’s Dismissal Policy
as outlined in the Treatment Consent. After 60 days of non-payment, Evolve Psychiatry reserves the right to pursue collection through
third-party agencies or legal remedies, including small claims court.

If a patient initiates an appeal with their insurance provider, they remain responsible for the outstanding balance unless and until the appeal is
approved. Payment is still expected within the standard 30-day period unless other arrangements have been made and documented.
