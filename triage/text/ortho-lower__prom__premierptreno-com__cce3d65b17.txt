                                   The Lower Extremity Functional Scale (LEFS)
Overview: The Lower Extremity Functional Scale (LEFS) can be used to evaluate the functional impairment of a patient with a disorder of one or both lower
extremities. It can be used to monitor the patient over time and to evaluate the effectiveness of an intervention. The authors are from McMaster University in
Hamilton, Ontario.
