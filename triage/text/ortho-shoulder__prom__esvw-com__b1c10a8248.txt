                                                                                             Sponsorship Opportunities
ASES Foundation’s 2026 National Shoulder and Elbow Week is an
opportunity to increase awareness nationally about the impact
that shoulder and elbow health care has on our society.

A top priority for the ASES Foundation is to build financial
channels for philanthropic donors to support the ongoing efforts
of surgeon educators and researchers so that we may continue
to provide high quality and cutting-edge care for our
communities.

Continuing impact - The incredibly successful 2025 National Shoulder and Elbow Week
hosted events in 8 major cities generating over $900,000 in funding from over 50 industry
supporters. Thank you! 50% of net proceeds will go back to the local communities for
shoulder and elbow research and education.

2026 National Shoulder and Elbow Week has selected 8 cities across North America to
host events May 11 -16, 2026. These evening events are held at unique venues, with
industry leading academic speakers, interesting guest speakers, and innovative industry
products enjoyed by either recipients or providers of shoulder and elbow care.

Goals of ASES Foundation’s LIFT UP National Shoulder and Elbow Week are:
• Promote prevention of shoulder & elbow injuries
• Disseminate information to patients about shoulder and elbow surgeons and their work
• Build a network of shoulder and elbow health professionals in local communities
• Learn latest discoveries, innovative treatments and products in shoulder & elbow surgery

Attendees are comprised of all reaches of the profession from patients, local community
leaders, industry partners and local community providers including top surgeons,
therapists, PAs, and nurses. Over 900 attendees will connect and learn about the latest
advancements in shoulder and elbow care through educational sessions and discussions.

History: The 2021 National Shoulder and Elbow Week was the first and held at five
locations. It is organized and managed by the ASES Foundation’s Committee on Patient
Centered Initiatives. The committee's mandate is to engage patients which is primarily
accomplished with National Shoulder and Elbow Week and educate them with patient-
centered materials on the ASES Foundation website.

Industry Partners make National Shoulder and Elbow Week possible through their
generous support. Consider being a national sponsor across all 8 locations. Sponsor here.
ASES members, support our profession by attending a local event or donating.
